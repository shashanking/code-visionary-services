The Problem

Real Estate Brokerages Struggle With

Lead Management

Lead Chaos

Leads pour in from WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn simultaneously. Without a system, most leads go cold before anyone follows up.

Operations

Zero Agent Visibility

Managers have no idea where agents are, who is working, or whether follow-ups are happening. Agent accountability is near impossible to enforce.

Finance

Commission Disputes

Deals involve installment payments from developers. Tracking who gets paid how much, and when, is done in spreadsheets — leading to errors and disputes.

Regulatory

Compliance Gaps

RERA Cards and Emirates IDs expire. Agents continue working with expired documents. There is no automated enforcement or alert system.

The Solution

One Platform. Three Layers.

Web CRM

Command Centre

Full-featured CRM for Admin, Managers, Finance, and Office staff. Leads, deals, finance, compliance, and fleet — all in one dark-mode dashboard.

AI Agent

Compliance Enforcement

Automated Regulatory Compliance

Requirements

Documents Required

  • Signed Service Agreement (mandatory, no expiry)
  • RERA Card (mandatory within 7 days, has expiry)
  • Emirates ID (mandatory within 7 days, has expiry)
  • Other documents (optional)
Onboarding Rule

7-Day Window

  • Agent can be approved with Service Agreement only
  • RERA and Emirates ID must be uploaded within 7 days
  • Daily cron checks all active agents
  • Either document missing after 7 days triggers auto temp-ban
Ongoing Monitoring

Expiry Cycle

  • Admin or Office verifies docs with expiry date attached
  • Daily cron monitors all verified document expiries
  • Either document expires — account temp-banned
  • Agent sees upload screen on next login
  • After re-upload — waiting approval again
Threat Detection

Lead Leakage

  • AI detects when agent shares own contact with a lead
  • Alert sent to Manager and Admin instantly
  • Leakage registered in the security log
  • Agent account auto temp-banned immediately
  • Agent must contact admin to restore access
